At its essence, origami is the traditional Japanese game of folding elaborately designed paper into
a myriad of shapes, typically plants, animals, and other living things. Part of what
makes origami unique is the fact that it simply requires a piece of paper and a creative imagination.

The traditional art of folding paper into attractive shapes to make decorative objects is called Origami.
